1. How long does shipping take?
All orders go through a production window of 3-5 business days before they ship, since everything is made to order. US customers typically receive their order within 5-10 business days total from the date of purchase.

2. Can I customize a design?
Yes - send us a message through Etsy and tell us what you have in mind. We welcome custom requests and will do our best to build something that fits your vision.

3. What if my item arrives damaged?
Contact us within 14 days of delivery with a photo of the damage and we'll take care of it. Replacements or exchanges are available for any item that arrives defective or damaged in transit.

4. Do you ship internationally?
Yes, international shipping is available to most destinations. Delivery times vary by country, and tracking is provided once your order ships.

5. What sizes are available?
T-shirts are available in a wide range of sizes, typically from S through 5XL depending on the style. Size charts are included on each product listing - we recommend checking them before ordering.

6. Are these designs AI-generated?
Some designs are developed with the assistance of AI-based tools, guided by concepts and direction drawn from real operational and technical backgrounds. We're transparent about that process and believe the context and intent behind each design is what gives it meaning.
