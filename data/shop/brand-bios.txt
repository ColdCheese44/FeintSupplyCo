SHORT
Feint Supply Co. is a veteran and former law enforcement owned print-on-demand shop making intelligence-inspired goods for analysts, investigators, veterans, and people who work in the noise. Made to order, built with intent.

ABOUT_SECTION
Feint Supply Co. exists because there is a specific kind of person who has spent time in high-pressure, low-margin environments -- military, law enforcement, cybersecurity, investigations -- and that person deserves goods that reflect where they have actually been, not a costume version of it. This shop was built for them.

The name comes from operational strategy. A feint is a deliberate move that controls attention and creates advantage. It is a concept that shows up in infantry tactics, in investigative technique, in incident response when you have to decide in real time what matters and what is distraction. That principle runs through every design in this shop -- signal over noise, quiet competence over performance.

Everything here is made to order by a veteran and former law enforcement founder who has worked the environments these designs reference. The aesthetic is earned, the humor is dry, and nothing in this catalog is here to look tough for an audience. If you have done the work, you will recognize it.

DARK_HUMOR
There is a certain kind of person who has sat in a surveillance vehicle for six hours eating gas station food, or stared at a SIEM dashboard at 3 a.m. watching alerts that turned out to be nothing -- and then the one that was not nothing. That person develops a specific relationship with humor. Not the kind that performs for a crowd. The kind that keeps the lights on.

Feint Supply Co. was built by one of those people. Army infantry, detective work, private investigations, cybersecurity operations -- a career that reads like a threat model with too many attack surfaces. The dark humor in this shop comes from that place. It is not edgy for the sake of it. It is what happens when you have processed enough chaos to find the part of it that is actually funny.

If you have ever written an after-action report on something you cannot fully explain, or filed evidence on a case that made you question your life choices, or responded to an incident that turned out to be a misconfigured alert at 2 a.m. -- this shop was made for you. The rest of you are welcome too, as long as you can keep up.

PROFESSIONAL
Feint Supply Co. was founded by Brendan Dodd, a U.S. Army Infantry veteran with subsequent careers in law enforcement investigations, licensed private investigation, and cybersecurity operations as a SOC analyst. His professional background spans criminal casework, digital evidence, fraud analysis, threat detection, and incident response -- fields that share a common operating principle: read the signal, cut the noise, and act with intent. That background informs every design direction and product decision in this shop.

MISSION_STATEMENT
Feint Supply Co. makes intelligence-inspired goods for people who have worked in the field -- veterans, analysts, investigators, and the civilians who share their operational sensibility. The mission is straightforward: build things that reflect real experience, skip the performance, and put something honest in front of people who can tell the difference.

Every item in this shop is made to order and designed with a specific kind of buyer in mind -- someone who has had to stay sharp under pressure, who understands that quiet competence is more useful than loud confidence, and who does not need a product to tell them they are tough. Feint Supply Co. is not a surplus store and it is not a tribute band. It is a small shop built on real background, making goods that hold up to scrutiny.

LISTING_FOOTER
Feint Supply Co. is a veteran and former law enforcement owned shop. Every item is made to order and printed when you buy it -- no warehouse stock, no filler. Designs are AI-assisted and human-directed by a founder who has worked the fields these goods reference. Built for people who know the difference between signal and noise.
