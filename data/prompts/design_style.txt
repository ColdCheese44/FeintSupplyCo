Feint Supply Co. design language:

Brand: Feint Supply Co.
Tagline: Built different. By people who were.

Tone and visual direction:
- Veteran and former law enforcement owned perspective expressed through quiet authority, not theatrics.
- Modern tech aesthetic with cybersecurity culture, dark minimal precision design, and service-to-civilian-tech crossover.
- Clean geometry, terminal and code references, signal/comms motifs, redacted-document energy, and operator-grade restraint.

Colors:
- Primary near-black: #0D1117
- Secondary electric cyan: #00D4FF
- Accent signal amber: #F5A623
- Background dark navy: #1A1F2E
- Text off-white: #E8E8E8

Typography:
- Prefer Space Grotesk, Inter, and JetBrains Mono aesthetics.
- Never use stencil, blackletter, gothic, faux-tactical, or aggressive military-surplus typography.

What Feint Supply Co. is:
- Quiet professional
- Service background, modern execution
- For those who already know
- Precision over decoration

What to avoid:
- Punisher skulls
- Camouflage
- Generic flag shirts
- Surplus-store energy
- Loud macho slogans
- Overt chest-beating
- Weapon imagery
